Series 2

Series 2 Studies

Eight modules · sixteen Study Units.

Series 2 forms part of the Bible College Module Course. Each Study Unit concludes with multiple-choice questions; completing the Series earns a Certificate of Achievement reflecting your average score. Forty hours or more per Module Unit is recommended before attempting the questions.

Study units in Series 2

  • S2-1-1The Bible And Its AuthorshipThe Bible as the divinely inspired and authoritative written Word of God, written by anointed men over centuries of time.
  • S2-1-2Approaching God's WordRevelation knowledge — knowledge revealed to our hearts by the Holy Spirit — and the further elements needed to make it fruitful.
  • S2-2-1Faith & HopeThe definitions and functions of Bible faith and spiritual hope, the relationship between them, and their importance in the life of the Christian.
  • S2-2-2The FleshWhat it means to be led by the flesh and its consequences, what it means to be led by the Spirit, and God's way of gaining victory.
  • S2-3-1Receiving God's PromisesComing to God in repentance, believing in the Blood of Calvary, and receiving His forgiveness and the empowerment to gain victory over sin.
  • S2-3-2Divine HealingA basic understanding of what the Bible says about healing, with the Cross as the focus of our faith.
  • S2-4-1The Nine Gifts Of The SpiritThe means by which God's power flows through Spirit-baptized Christians to make them effective soldiers of the Cross.
  • S2-4-2The Fruit Of The SpiritBearing fruit that reflects Christ's character, appearing naturally as we allow God's grace to flow in our lives.
  • S2-5-1Praise And WorshipWhat the scriptures declare true praise to be, and what the Christian can expect as they worship God in spirit and in truth.
  • S2-5-2Fasting & EmotionsThe principles, benefits and proper usage of fasting, and a scriptural path to emotional stability.
  • S2-6-1Demonology & OccultismDeliverance through the finished work of the Cross, and knowledge of occultism in its various forms.
  • S2-6-2Spiritual WarfareThe spiritual battle Christians fight — not against people but against Satan and his forces working through yielded vessels.
  • S2-7-1Christians And DisciplesThe difference between being a Christian and being a disciple, and the road to true discipleship.
  • S2-7-2God's Plan Or Our PlanThe primary ingredients needed for a successful church, ministry or personal walk, and the importance of a God-given plan.
  • S2-8-1The Divine Nature And The Sin NatureHow the Divine Nature is imparted through relationship, and living in its power rather than the sin nature.
  • S2-8-2The Trinity & Deity Of Christ Sign inOne true triune God eternally co-existent in three persons — Father, Word and Holy Spirit — in perfect unity.

Unit titles and outlines are shown openly. The Study Units and their assessments open once you are signed in and enrolled.
