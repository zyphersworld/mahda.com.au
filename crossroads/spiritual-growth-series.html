Spiritual Growth Series

Foundational truths of God's Word for new Christians.

With many teaching booklets covering an extensive array of doctrines — accessible through hyperlinks in our online Crusade Booklet — this is a valuable resource for spiritual growth. The Spiritual Growth Series offers a six-session outline, which can be extended or tailored to your teaching needs, as a guide for teaching new Christians the foundational truths of God's Word.

This provides much-needed spiritual nourishment and helps new believers grow, become stable, and strengthen their relationship with the Lord. People learn progressively — line upon line, precept upon precept (Isaiah 28:10) — and this carefully designed booklet creates that opportunity.

The six-session outline

  • 01Assurance of Salvation
  • 02The Word of God
  • 03Prayer
  • 04The Holy Spirit
  • 05Water Baptism & the Church
  • 06Living the Christian Life Booklet — sign in

The outline above is a suggested guide and may be extended or adapted. The linked teaching booklets open once you are signed in.

The Spiritual Growth Series is the recommended starting point for new believers.
