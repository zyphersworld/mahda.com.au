Curriculum

The Full Curriculum

The complete module curriculum across all three Series.

The full curriculum for the Bible College Module Course, across three Series. Each Study Unit is coded (Series–Module–Unit) and concludes with a multiple-choice assessment. Module titles and outlines are shown openly; the Study Units and assessments open once you are signed in and enrolled.

Series One

Eight Modules

  • S1-1-1Salvation MessageA brief outline of the salvation message — Christianity founded on and nurtured through a relationship with the Father, about the grace of God through which we are saved.
  • S1-2-1Christian FoundationsAn outline of what is required of you as a Christian, and how to begin claiming your rightful inheritance as a child of the living God.
  • S1-3-1CommunionThe purpose of communion, what it represents, the requirements for partaking, and the benefits it provides — one of the two New Covenant ordinances.
  • S1-4-1Water BaptismOne of the commands necessary to our obedience to Christ. Its purpose in the life of the Christian, important to your spiritual growth and walk with the Lord.
  • S1-5-1Baptism In The Holy Spirit And With FireIt is impossible to overestimate the importance of being baptized in the Holy Spirit if Christians are to walk effectively in their callings.
  • S1-6-1Our Prayer Life & The Blood CoveringPrayer as a means to communicate and fellowship with God, and the right use of the blood covering in effective, properly anchored prayer.
  • S1-7-1HealingWhat the Bible says concerning sickness, disease, healing and health, and the mistake of substituting belief in healing for the receiving of God's grace.
  • S1-8-1Tithes & OfferingsAcknowledging that all we have belongs to God, and giving as He has instructed in His Word rather than as we feel.
Series Two

Sixteen Study Units

  • S2-1-1The Bible And Its AuthorshipThe Bible as the divinely inspired and authoritative written Word of God, written by anointed men over centuries of time.
  • S2-1-2Approaching God's WordRevelation knowledge — knowledge revealed to our hearts by the Holy Spirit — and the further elements needed to make it fruitful.
  • S2-2-1Faith & HopeThe definitions and functions of Bible faith and spiritual hope, the relationship between them, and their importance in the life of the Christian.
  • S2-2-2The FleshWhat it means to be led by the flesh and its consequences, what it means to be led by the Spirit, and God's way of gaining victory.
  • S2-3-1Receiving God's PromisesComing to God in repentance, believing in the Blood of Calvary, and receiving His forgiveness and the empowerment to gain victory over sin.
  • S2-3-2Divine HealingA basic understanding of what the Bible says about healing, with the Cross as the focus of our faith.
  • S2-4-1The Nine Gifts Of The SpiritThe means by which God's power flows through Spirit-baptized Christians to make them effective soldiers of the Cross.
  • S2-4-2The Fruit Of The SpiritBearing fruit that reflects Christ's character, appearing naturally as we allow God's grace to flow in our lives.
  • S2-5-1Praise And WorshipWhat the scriptures declare true praise to be, and what the Christian can expect as they worship God in spirit and in truth.
  • S2-5-2Fasting & EmotionsThe principles, benefits and proper usage of fasting, and a scriptural path to emotional stability.
  • S2-6-1Demonology & OccultismDeliverance through the finished work of the Cross, and knowledge of occultism in its various forms.
  • S2-6-2Spiritual WarfareThe spiritual battle Christians fight — not against people but against Satan and his forces working through yielded vessels.
  • S2-7-1Christians And DisciplesThe difference between being a Christian and being a disciple, and the road to true discipleship.
  • S2-7-2God's Plan Or Our PlanThe primary ingredients needed for a successful church, ministry or personal walk, and the importance of a God-given plan.
  • S2-8-1The Divine Nature And The Sin NatureHow the Divine Nature is imparted through relationship, and living in its power rather than the sin nature.
  • S2-8-2The Trinity & Deity Of ChristOne true triune God eternally co-existent in three persons — Father, Word and Holy Spirit — in perfect unity.
Series Three

Twenty-two Study Units

  • S3-1-1The Fivefold MinistryThe offices of Apostle, Prophet, Evangelist, Pastor and Teacher, and why these callings are given to the Body of Christ.
  • S3-1-2The AnointingHow vital the anointing of God is — bringing conviction, drawing souls, healing, delivering and teaching, inseparable from the Holy Spirit.
  • S3-2-1The Story Of CreationGenesis as the book of beginnings, and the authentic story of creation over against the theory of evolution.
  • S3-2-2The Fall & Redemption Of ManThe account of Adam and Eve and the Tree of Life, and man's redemption through the Blood of Jesus.
  • S3-3-1The Baptism Of RegenerationBeing 'born again' — what happened spiritually at salvation, and God's purpose in creating man to fellowship with Himself.
  • S3-3-2Hell And The AfterlifeOur spiritual position before salvation likened to a criminal sentenced for life, and the separation sin brings from God's society.
  • S3-4-1Pentecostal Vs Charismatic Part 1Addressing the heresies flourishing today and diverting Christians from sound Biblical truth.
  • S3-4-2Pentecostal Vs Charismatic Part 2The different kinds of false teaching the Church has embraced in these last days, and the wind of the Spirit bringing revelation.
  • S3-5-1The Covenant Of God Part 1A covenant as a formal binding contract in which each party pledges to perform certain actions.
  • S3-5-2The Covenant Of God Part 2A covenant as a legal agreement — a will or testament with conditions both parties must fulfil.
  • S3-6-1The Law Part 1Which parts of Old Covenant law have been abolished and which remain, and how law still applies in New Testament times.
  • S3-6-2The Law Part 2How only perfect Blood could satisfy God's justice and redeem man, God the Word becoming man to pay the ransom price.
  • S3-7-1Everything Is Governed By LawsGod's laws as absolute and unchangeable, enforced by His power as a governing force when the conditions are met.
  • S3-7-2God's Purpose In Revealing The LawThe Mosaic Law as God's comprehensive blueprint for living according to His righteous standards.
  • S3-8-1The False Doctrine Of Unconditional Eternal SecurityBasic Biblical truths to enlighten Christians so they may walk in freedom and victory rather than bondage.
  • S3-8-2Marriage, Divorce & RemarriageLaying the foundation of the Law of Love to minister godly counsel and wisdom through the complexities of this subject.
  • S3-9-1Rapture Of The Church Part 1A brief overview of end-time events — the Rapture, Tribulation, Antichrist, Restoration of Israel, Second Coming and Millennial Kingdom.
  • S3-9-2Rapture Of The Church Part 2The history of Israel as God's chosen race, and the ingrafting of the Gentiles as a wild olive branch onto the true root.
  • S3-10-1End Times — The TribulationThe seven-year Tribulation divided into two parts, and the 144,000 Jewish converts who will proclaim the Gospel.
  • S3-10-2End Times — The AntichristThe meaning of 'Antichrist' — one who stands against and opposes Christ and all He stands for.
  • S3-11-1End Times — Nebuchadnezzar's DreamThe account in Daniel 2 of Nebuchadnezzar's image, revealing the succession of future world empires.
  • S3-11-2End Times — Zechariah's VisionThe prophetic writings of Zechariah, examined as 'history in advance,' proving the accuracy of Biblical prophecy.
