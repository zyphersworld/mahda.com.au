Statement of Faith

Our Beliefs

The doctrines we hold and teach.

Crossroads stands within the stream of historic classical Pentecostal faith and affirms the core biblical and Pentecostal doctrines commonly recognised within the Australian Christian Churches (A.C.C.). The following sets forth the fundamental beliefs we hold and teach.

The Eternal Godhead

We believe in God, who is eternal and self-existent, revealed as one Being in three Persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — and the Creator of all things.

The Lord Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ, the eternal Son of God, conceived of the Holy Spirit and born of the Virgin Mary. He lived a sinless life, was crucified and died on our behalf, rose from the dead, and ascended into heaven, where He is exalted as the risen Lord and Intercessor.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit, fully divine, who proceeds from the Father and the Son. He convicts, regenerates and sanctifies, and guides believers into all truth.

The Holy Bible

We believe that the Bible is the inerrant, inspired, and infallible Word of God and our highest authority for faith and practice.

Creation

We believe that God created the universe, and that all people are created in the image of God, to love and worship Him.

The Devil

We believe in the existence and person of the devil, who actively opposes the purposes of God.

The Fall of Humanity

We believe that all people have rejected God through their sinful acts and are separated from God.

The Atonement

We believe that salvation is found in Jesus Christ alone who, by His suffering, shed blood, death on the cross, and resurrection, has made full atonement for the sins of all people. We receive salvation by grace through repentance and faith in Jesus Christ.

The Church

We believe that the Church, the Body of Christ, is the community who confess faith in Jesus Christ. While universal, the Church is local in expression and witness, existing for worship, fellowship, evangelism, discipleship, and mission.

Water Baptism

We believe in water baptism by single immersion in the Name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit — a public declaration of faith in, and identification with, the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

The Lord's Supper

We believe in the Lord's Supper, in symbolic remembrance of the sacrifice of Jesus Christ until He returns.

Sanctification

We believe in the regenerating work of the Holy Spirit, who convicts of sin and inspires repentance and faith, transforming believers into the likeness of Jesus Christ.

The Baptism in the Holy Spirit

We believe in the Baptism of the Holy Spirit — an experience distinct from and subsequent to salvation, available to all believers, accompanied by the initial evidence of speaking in tongues, resulting in empowerment for witness.

Gifts of the Holy Spirit

We believe that the Holy Spirit imparts spiritual gifts to believers for ministry to one another, the Church, and the world.

Divine Healing

We believe that God heals today through the atoning work of Jesus Christ. By praying in faith for healing and miracles, we continue the ministry of Jesus Christ.

The Second Coming of Christ

We believe in the imminent and personal return of Jesus Christ to gather His people to Himself and to judge the living and the dead. His Kingdom will have no end.

The Punishment of the Wicked

We believe in the resurrection of the body. All who believe in Jesus Christ will have everlasting life in the presence of God; those who reject Him will have eternal separation from God.
