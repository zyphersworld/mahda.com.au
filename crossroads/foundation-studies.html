Foundation Studies

Seven Study Units building a strong doctrinal foundation.

The Foundation Studies Course consists of seven Study Units designed to build a strong biblical understanding of foundational doctrine. Each unit deepens knowledge and faith, and includes a guided review where students select correct answers to reinforce their understanding.

After completing all seven Study Units, students receive a Certificate of Completion. This course provides a solid framework for all who seek to establish or renew their understanding of essential spiritual principles.

The seven Study Units

  • 01Knowing God & His Word
  • 02Salvation & the New Life
  • 03Repentance & Faith
  • 04Water Baptism
  • 05The Baptism in the Holy Spirit
  • 06Prayer & the Life of Worship
  • 07The Church & Christian Living Assessment — sign in

Recommended study

Dedicate twenty hours or more to each of the seven units before attempting the questions. The College booklet Studying helps students maximise their learning.
