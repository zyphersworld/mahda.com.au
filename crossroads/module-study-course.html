Level 4

Bible College Module Course

In-depth theological study across three Series.

The fourth level offers the in-depth Bible College Module Course, online, structured into three Series with multiple-choice questions at the end of every Study Unit. Each completed Series earns a Certificate of Achievement reflecting the average score; all three earn a Certificate in Christian Studies.

As students progress, the Module Series offers a structured pathway to deeper learning — beneficial for those young in the faith, older Christians, and those in or aspiring to ministry. Forty hours or more per Module Unit is devoted to study before attempting the questions.

Certificate of Completion in Theological Foundations
Awarded upon completion of Series 1, Series 2, and Series 3 individually.
Advanced Diploma of Theology
Granted upon completion of all assessments across Series 1–3. Internal, non-accredited award comparable to AQF Level 6.
