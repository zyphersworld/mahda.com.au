The booklets · 70 titles

Approaching God's Word

God's Word has the answer to every problem we will ever face, for it leads us into all truth. But we need to seek Him and His Word with a sincere and honest heart, ridding ourselves of biased attitudes and preconceived ideas in order to clearly understand what God is saying.

Baptism In The Holy Spirit

It is impossible to overestimate the importance of being baptized in the Holy Spirit. This teaching covers the questions people often raise concerning the Baptism in the Holy Spirit, reaffirming what God's Word really teaches on the matter.

Baptism In The Holy Spirit And Fire

Like other vital doctrines, the Baptism in the Holy Spirit has been under much attack from the enemy. This booklet answers the confusion, doubt and wrong teaching surrounding Spirit Baptism so that people may know the fullness and power of the Holy Spirit.

Baptism Of Regeneration

About the term 'the baptism of regeneration' — more widely known as being 'born again.' Though every Christian has been through this experience, many have no real concept of what happened to them spiritually when they were saved.

Biblical Hermeneutics

Christian Doctrine may be defined as the fundamental Truths of the Bible arranged in systematic form. This study — commonly called Theology — deals with our knowledge of God and His relations to man, according to His Word.

Christians And Disciples

Today the words 'disciple' and 'Christian' can have quite different meanings. Being a Christian does not automatically make us a disciple of Christ. This booklet explores the road to true discipleship.

Communion

The celebration of communion is an extremely important aspect of Christian worship. This study focuses on its purpose, what it represents, the requirements for partaking, and the benefits it provides.

Covenant Of Atonement

Through sinning, Adam placed himself and all his offspring in the position of a criminal, losing the rights heaven had to offer. This study traces the covenant by which atonement was made.

Covenant Of Redemption

The Old Testament sacrifices needed to be repeated day after day. But Calvary's holy perfect sacrifice was a once-and-for-all completed sacrifice, and through this Atonement, redemption was made possible.

Demonology Part One

Demons appear to have originated from a creation before man. Scripture shows that ever since the fall of Adam, a spiritual battle has been raging for the possession of men's minds, bodies and spirits.

Discerning The Lord's Body For Healing

Calvary's holy perfect sacrifice put an end to all sacrifices by fulfilling the law of sacrifice for man. This study shows how discerning the Lord's body relates to healing.

Discerning Truth From Error

This teaching helps you discern between good and evil, that which is of God and that which is false. We need to be thoroughly grounded in God's Word so as not to be led astray by what someone thinks or feels.

Divine Healing

Helps Christians gain a basic understanding of what the Bible says about healing, and how God's principles of healing can be applied to their lives. The focus of our faith must always be the Cross.

Emotions

Society has taught man to own his emotional thoughts, as though emotions define who we are. This study offers a scriptural path to emotional stability and dealing with the demands of emotions.

Eschatology and Biblical Hermeneutics

The Book of Revelation reveals Jesus as the Prince of Peace, the King of kings who will triumph over all. The major theme of Revelation is Jesus — the glorified Christ, His victories and His eternal Kingdom.

Everything Is Governed By Laws

God's law had been broken by man's sin, and only perfect Blood could satisfy God's justice and redeem man. This study examines how everything is governed by spiritual laws higher than natural laws.

Faith And Hope

Spiritual hope is meant to be an anchor for the soul. This study shows how hope precedes faith, giving us the boundaries of God's Word so that faith may develop through trust.

Fasting

Fasting is an often misunderstood and neglected subject. For the Christian it is primarily a tool to aid us in our walk — not a ritual. This study looks at its principles, benefits, and proper usage.

Fruit Of The Spirit

Bearing fruit is a significant part of the Christian walk. The fruit in our lives should reflect Christ's character and appear naturally as we allow God's grace to flow, not manufactured through self-effort.

Getting To Know Your Bible

Don't let its size frighten you. The Bible is filled with adventure, heroes and ordinary people just like you — and it has the answers to life's most important questions.

God's Absolute Law

Establishes which parts of Old Covenant law have been abolished and which remain today, how 'law' still applies in New Testament times, and the benefits of obeying it.

God's Plan Or Our Plan

Examines the requirements for churches, ministries and individuals to achieve success in the Lord, and how vital it is to have a God-given plan to work towards the goal to which God has called us.

God's Purpose In Revealing The Law

In Old Testament times God revealed His principles to Israel through the Mosaic Law. This study explains God's purpose in revealing the law, and how His moral principles are written on men's hearts.

God's Law Is Contained Within The Conscience Of Man

The rest of the world is under a law that operates through the conscience of man. From the beginning, mankind was created with a conscience which, if allowed, would convict him in regard to sin.

Healing

Have you been told that sickness is God-given, intended to refine character? Only by examining the divinely inspired words of the Bible can we obtain a true picture of God's will concerning sickness and health.

Hell And The Afterlife

The world of departed spirits, known as 'Sheol' or 'Hades.' Using the account of Lazarus and the rich man, this study examines the two sections of the underworld and the accountability of man.

Law Of Love

Because we are now under the law of love and have direct access to God, certain limitations experienced in Old Testament times no longer apply. The Blood of Christ deals a mortal blow to sin's power.

Laying On Of Hands And The Blood Covering

Laying hands on people when praying is scriptural, but spiritual doorways can open between participants. This study teaches how to focus on what God will do for those who believe and trust in Him.

Love And Lust

Love and lust are spiritual opposites — true love gives for the benefit of others, whereas lust seeks to fulfil its own desires. This study examines both, and how to walk in the love in which Jesus walked.

Law Or Grace

Christians are saved by grace, not by trying to earn God's favour through the Law. Yet we still need to conform to God's righteous standards — not through willpower but through Divine empowerment.

Marriage, Divorce And Remarriage

Presents Biblical truth on this often-misunderstood subject so that people may be delivered from bondage. As truth is accepted and acted upon, it brings freedom (John 8:32).

Nine Gifts Of The Spirit

When Christians are baptized in the Holy Spirit, they are clothed with power from on high. The gifts of the Spirit are the means by which God's power flows, as the Spirit sees fit (1 Corinthians 12:11).

Now That You're Saved

Congratulations on receiving Jesus as Lord and Saviour — the most important decision of your life. This booklet lays out the basic Christian truths for the new believer, from assurance of salvation to growing in the Lord.

Occult Oppression & Bondage

Brings a basic knowledge of occultism in its various forms, including subtle activities people accept as harmless. Occultism is any involvement with astrology, fortune-telling, magic practices or spiritism.

Our Prayer Life

Prayer is a means by which we communicate and fellowship with God. Through prayer we seek direction, guidance, empowerment and enlightenment, becoming more aligned with our Father's will.

Pentecostal Verses Charismatic Part 1

Addresses heresies currently flourishing and diverting Christians from sound Biblical truth. Many churches have become Charismatic in nature while remaining Pentecostal in name.

Pentecostal Verses Charismatic Part 2

Describes the different kinds of false teaching the Church world has embraced in these last days, and the strong wind of the Spirit blowing to bring revelation and the power of God into the Church.

Power Of A Transformed Mind

After salvation, you need to renew your mind through studying the Word and anointed teaching. While your spirit is made pure at salvation, your mind takes time to bring into line with your position.

Praise And Worship

Outlines what the scriptures declare true praise to be, and what the Christian can expect as they seek to worship God 'in spirit and in truth' (John 4:23-24).

Rapture Of The Church

The 'snatching away' of all living Saints to join the Lord in the air (1 Thessalonians 4:16-17), followed by the Tribulation and the Battle of Armageddon.

Reason For Unanswered Prayers

A compiled list of some of the possible reasons for unanswered prayer and attacks of the enemy, to help people who may be having difficulty receiving from God.

Receiving God's Promises

Many think we must put our lives in order before coming to God. But we can only put our lives in order by coming to God in repentance, believing in the Blood of Calvary, and receiving His empowerment.

Salvation Message

A brief outline of the salvation message. Christianity is founded on and nurtured through a relationship between the Father and His son or daughter — about the grace of God through which we are saved.

Sin - Its Origin And Remedy

Adam, as man's federal head, sinned against God, transferring mankind's spiritual parentage to Satan and bringing spiritual death upon all. To become a son of God, each child of Adam must be 'born again.'

Spiritual Warfare

We do not have to succumb to the wiles of the enemy. The only power Satan retains in Christians' lives is that which they give him. It is God's grace working through us that defeats the enemy.

Studying The Bible

The greatest sign that a teaching is of God is God's convicting power on the Word when spoken in wisdom and faith. This booklet teaches how to discern truth and study effectively.

Tests And Trials

We are not promised exemption from tests and trials; God's Word tells us our faith will be tested. Jesus experienced everything we endure and is supremely qualified to help us in our times of need.

The Anointing Of God

How vital the anointing of God is. It is by His Anointing that the Lord brings conviction, draws souls into the Kingdom, heals, delivers and teaches. The Anointing and the Holy Spirit are inseparable.

The Bible And Its Authorship

The Bible is the divinely inspired and authoritative written Word of God — a library of 66 books, 39 in the Old Testament and 27 in the New, written by anointed men over centuries of time.

The Blood Covering

The 'blood-covering prayer' is often used wrongly, as though it gives magic protection regardless of sin. This study examines the theology behind it and when and how it can be rightly used.

The Covenant Of God Part 1

A covenant is a formal binding contract in which each party pledges to perform certain actions. This study assumes blessing if the covenant is honoured and a curse if it is broken.

The Covenant Of God Part 2

The Bible is divided into the Old and New Covenants, which together describe the complete Covenant of God between mankind and Himself — a legal agreement with conditions both parties must fulfil.

The Cross

The shed Blood of Christ is found at the Cross, where the price was paid. The legal union of a Believer and Jesus comes only through the Cross, progressing into a daily relationship where God and man commune.

The Divine Nature And The Sin Nature

At regeneration, all pollution is removed from our spirit and the Divine Nature is imparted, giving us the potential to love as God loves. We must will for this to happen by acting on God's Word.

The False Doctrine of Unconditional Eternal Security

Clouded by misinformation and scriptural ignorance, this subject needs the light of God's Word. Presents basic Biblical truths so Christians may walk in freedom and victory rather than bondage.

The Fivefold Ministry

Our churches lack the operation of the Fivefold Ministry offices — Apostle, Prophet, Evangelist, Pastor and Teacher — given for the perfecting of the saints, the work of the ministry, and the edifying of the Body.

The Flesh

Helps us understand the scriptural term 'the flesh' — what it means to be led by the flesh and its consequences, what it means to be led by the Spirit, and God's way of gaining victory over the flesh.

The Kingdom Of God

Since the creation of man it has been God's desire to establish a physical Kingdom on earth, governed by Himself — a theocracy over which God Himself rules, in and through man.

The Mosaic Covenant

Between the Fall and Moses, various blood Covenants held eternal death at bay. God called Abram to found His holy nation, giving special promises of an everlasting Covenant and a coming Messiah.

The Priesthood Of God

Because we have a High Priest seated at the right hand of the Father, we can come boldly to the throne of grace. As our Mediator, Jesus is our eternal High Priest — the Priesthood fulfilled in Him.

The Story Of Creation Pt. 1

Genesis is a book of beginnings. 'In the beginning God' gives us the reason all creation came into being. Evolution denies the Creator; from Genesis we learn the authentic story of creation.

The Story of Creation Pt. 2

Building on Part One, this study examines the vast untold story between Genesis 1:1 and Genesis 1:2, and God's masterpiece of creation pieced together from a careful examination of scripture.

The Tree Of Life

When God said man would die the day he ate of the tree of the knowledge of good and evil, He referred to spiritual death — separation from Him — with physical death following as a consequence of the Fall.

The Trinity & Deity Of Christ

The term 'Trinity' denotes the Christian doctrine that God is a unity of three persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit — one triune God, eternally co-existent, dwelling together in perfect unity.

The Wrath Of God

The wrath of God is a much-misunderstood subject — a function of God's holy character expressed through judgement. God is a God of great mercy, but also of justice, and He will always fulfil His Word.

Tithes And Offerings

As we enter our new life in Christ, God becomes our provider. In giving tithes and offerings we acknowledge that all we have belongs to God, and that our reliance is on Him and His Word.

To Drink Or Not To Drink

Whether Christians should drink alcohol has long been debated. The Bible tells us not to be drunk or in excess, but to be filled with the Spirit — controlled by the Spirit of God and nothing else.

Water Baptism

For the Christian, water baptism is like a wedding ceremony — a vow of commitment presented to the world, oneself, the enemy and the sin nature, and a symbolic demonstration of death to the old man.

Water Baptism - tract

Congratulations on beginning your new life in Christ. A short introduction to water baptism and its purpose in the life of the new believer, encouraging obedience to Christ's command.

Why Does God Allow War And Suffering To Continue

Returns to the subject of the lease God gave man over the earth. Man is a free-willed agent; violation of God's principles brings a curse that may manifest as wars, famines and drought.
