Awards

Certificates & Diploma Awards

Recognition of achievement across every level of study.

Crossroads awards are internal, non-accredited recognitions of study and achievement, valued by many churches and mission organisations for ministry purposes.

Foundation

Certificate of Completion in Christian Foundation Studies
Awarded upon completion of the seven Foundation Studies.

Module Course

Certificate of Completion in Theological Foundations
Awarded upon completion of Series 1, Series 2, and Series 3 individually.
Advanced Diploma of Theology
Granted upon completion of all assessments across Series 1–3. Comparable to AQF Level 6.

How performance is measured

Assessments are multiple-choice, evaluated by percentage. Certificates reflect the student's average score. Responses must reflect the content presented in the Study Units and booklets.
