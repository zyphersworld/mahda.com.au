About the College

Since its establishment in 1990 in Victoria, Australia, Crossroads International Bible College has played a role in equipping Christians and in developing and nurturing ministers for Christian ministry. Although similar to numerous Bible colleges, it does not possess accreditation and does not actively pursue recognition from national accrediting agencies. Nonetheless, individuals pursuing personal development or ministry training find real value in the courses offered. Many churches, denominations, and mission organisations acknowledge and accept qualifications from Crossroads as valid credentials for ministry.

Crossroads is part of the Full Gospel International Church, an independent denomination affiliated with the worldwide World Evangelism Fellowship, which has thousands of affiliated churches.
