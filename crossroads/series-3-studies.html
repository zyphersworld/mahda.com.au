Series 3

Series 3 Studies

Eleven modules · twenty-two Study Units.

Series 3 forms part of the Bible College Module Course. Each Study Unit concludes with multiple-choice questions; completing the Series earns a Certificate of Achievement reflecting your average score. Forty hours or more per Module Unit is recommended before attempting the questions.

Study units in Series 3

  • S3-1-1The Fivefold MinistryThe offices of Apostle, Prophet, Evangelist, Pastor and Teacher, and why these callings are given to the Body of Christ.
  • S3-1-2The AnointingHow vital the anointing of God is — bringing conviction, drawing souls, healing, delivering and teaching, inseparable from the Holy Spirit.
  • S3-2-1The Story Of CreationGenesis as the book of beginnings, and the authentic story of creation over against the theory of evolution.
  • S3-2-2The Fall & Redemption Of ManThe account of Adam and Eve and the Tree of Life, and man's redemption through the Blood of Jesus.
  • S3-3-1The Baptism Of RegenerationBeing 'born again' — what happened spiritually at salvation, and God's purpose in creating man to fellowship with Himself.
  • S3-3-2Hell And The AfterlifeOur spiritual position before salvation likened to a criminal sentenced for life, and the separation sin brings from God's society.
  • S3-4-1Pentecostal Vs Charismatic Part 1Addressing the heresies flourishing today and diverting Christians from sound Biblical truth.
  • S3-4-2Pentecostal Vs Charismatic Part 2The different kinds of false teaching the Church has embraced in these last days, and the wind of the Spirit bringing revelation.
  • S3-5-1The Covenant Of God Part 1A covenant as a formal binding contract in which each party pledges to perform certain actions.
  • S3-5-2The Covenant Of God Part 2A covenant as a legal agreement — a will or testament with conditions both parties must fulfil.
  • S3-6-1The Law Part 1Which parts of Old Covenant law have been abolished and which remain, and how law still applies in New Testament times.
  • S3-6-2The Law Part 2How only perfect Blood could satisfy God's justice and redeem man, God the Word becoming man to pay the ransom price.
  • S3-7-1Everything Is Governed By LawsGod's laws as absolute and unchangeable, enforced by His power as a governing force when the conditions are met.
  • S3-7-2God's Purpose In Revealing The LawThe Mosaic Law as God's comprehensive blueprint for living according to His righteous standards.
  • S3-8-1The False Doctrine Of Unconditional Eternal SecurityBasic Biblical truths to enlighten Christians so they may walk in freedom and victory rather than bondage.
  • S3-8-2Marriage, Divorce & RemarriageLaying the foundation of the Law of Love to minister godly counsel and wisdom through the complexities of this subject.
  • S3-9-1Rapture Of The Church Part 1A brief overview of end-time events — the Rapture, Tribulation, Antichrist, Restoration of Israel, Second Coming and Millennial Kingdom.
  • S3-9-2Rapture Of The Church Part 2The history of Israel as God's chosen race, and the ingrafting of the Gentiles as a wild olive branch onto the true root.
  • S3-10-1End Times — The TribulationThe seven-year Tribulation divided into two parts, and the 144,000 Jewish converts who will proclaim the Gospel.
  • S3-10-2End Times — The AntichristThe meaning of 'Antichrist' — one who stands against and opposes Christ and all He stands for.
  • S3-11-1End Times — Nebuchadnezzar's DreamThe account in Daniel 2 of Nebuchadnezzar's image, revealing the succession of future world empires.
  • S3-11-2End Times — Zechariah's Vision Sign inThe prophetic writings of Zechariah, examined as 'history in advance,' proving the accuracy of Biblical prophecy.

Unit titles and outlines are shown openly. The Study Units and their assessments open once you are signed in and enrolled.
