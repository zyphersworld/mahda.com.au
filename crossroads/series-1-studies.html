Series 1

Series 1 Studies

Eight modules · eight Study Units.

Series 1 forms part of the Bible College Module Course. Each Study Unit concludes with multiple-choice questions; completing the Series earns a Certificate of Achievement reflecting your average score. Forty hours or more per Module Unit is recommended before attempting the questions.

Study units in Series 1

  • S1-1-1Salvation MessageA brief outline of the salvation message — Christianity founded on and nurtured through a relationship with the Father, about the grace of God through which we are saved.
  • S1-2-1Christian FoundationsAn outline of what is required of you as a Christian, and how to begin claiming your rightful inheritance as a child of the living God.
  • S1-3-1CommunionThe purpose of communion, what it represents, the requirements for partaking, and the benefits it provides — one of the two New Covenant ordinances.
  • S1-4-1Water BaptismOne of the commands necessary to our obedience to Christ. Its purpose in the life of the Christian, important to your spiritual growth and walk with the Lord.
  • S1-5-1Baptism In The Holy Spirit And With FireIt is impossible to overestimate the importance of being baptized in the Holy Spirit if Christians are to walk effectively in their callings.
  • S1-6-1Our Prayer Life & The Blood CoveringPrayer as a means to communicate and fellowship with God, and the right use of the blood covering in effective, properly anchored prayer.
  • S1-7-1HealingWhat the Bible says concerning sickness, disease, healing and health, and the mistake of substituting belief in healing for the receiving of God's grace.
  • S1-8-1Tithes & Offerings Sign inAcknowledging that all we have belongs to God, and giving as He has instructed in His Word rather than as we feel.

Unit titles and outlines are shown openly. The Study Units and their assessments open once you are signed in and enrolled.
