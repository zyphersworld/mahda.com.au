The Reckoning Desk Tracking the insolvency of the Trustees of the Christian Brothers — and what it means for the people they were always meant to answer to.

On 22 June 2026, after 183 years in Australia, the Trustees of the Christian Brothers (Oceania Province) announced they would ask creditors and the Court to approve a Creditors' Scheme of Arrangement to wind up and distribute roughly $216 million in remaining assets. The creditors include the survivors of the abuse the order's members committed. This page is kept by one of them. It is not legal advice. It is a way of refusing to look away.
