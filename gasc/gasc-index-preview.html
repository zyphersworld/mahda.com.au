      ⚠First Classic, or first in a while? Gates open early — the infield fills fast and the good grandstand seats go first. Bring ear protection (these are 410ci methanol sprintcars, they are loud), sun cover for the afternoon, and something warm for when the sea breeze comes in after dark. Cash and card both work at the canteen. Camping books out well ahead — sort it before you drive down.
